An Ironsworn: Starforged solo playthrough

Session 1

Stardate 2407.04.03

Woden has been Living in Concord for awhile now sorting through finding of the past few adventures. Done with the research he finds out that Isla Nakayama doesn't live too far away and is at Beacon. He prepares his ship for another set of adventures and heads out. From Concord to Beacon is about 7.68 ly away, and with traveling about 1 ly per 2 cycles. Should take Woden about 15 cycles. He heads out.

Stardate 2407.04.18

Nothing eventful happened on the journey and Woden docks at the massive orbital station named Beacon. He and Tindra (Companion), make their way off the ship, now he must find where this place is that Isla Nakayama works at. He asks around for a little bit and finally gets some directions. The station is massive, so he rents a hover bike to get around faster. After a few stops to confirm he was going in the correct direction he finally makes it to StellarTron, Isla's company headquarters.

It was small, but fairly high class for a business. I walk in and ask the receptionist to tell Isla the her friend Woden was here, then she asks me to take a seat.

Session 0

Backstory

I am utterly fascinated with ancient things. I am a grub. I love exploring so much I rarely ever see my family.

Background Vow

Starship Frea

History

My parents worked hard and when I was old enough, they gifted me an old beat up, small, but working, starship.

Quirks

Exterior is marred by rust and grime. Gravity generator is notoriously fickle. Looks defenseless, but exterior panels open to reveal weapons.

Tindra, the Green Crystal Sprite

She is very helpful and nice. They found each other while she was on her home world doing some field research about some Precursor species. They taught each other their language and she grew very fond of him. Now she travels around with him as kind of an assistant, though she really can't do whole lot due to her size.

Woden's Appearance

Woden almost always looks disheveled, looking like scientist that partied too hard. He lost both his arms when he was younger thanks to an accident involving some machinery her was working on, now he has some basic cyborg arms. His coat and gloves cover his arms as some people stare.

Personal Gear

A rugged and powerful datapad with recording accessories. Iron totem of a raven.

Summary

Went through the entire process of setting up in the book. Woden is headed to Beacon to meet up with an old friend they he hasn't seen in a very long time, since they were kids. Isla Nakayama worked her way to owning her own company, small but profitable. Her main focus is working on old precursor tech. This time she has figured out an old power source of those ancient times, she thinks she has figured it out and has put it up for sale. The Hunters of the Golden Alliance is coming to examine the power source to buy the research from her.
