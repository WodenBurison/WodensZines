Description

Holland is a rocky world in the outer Kalidas Maw. From orbit you can pick it out by the plateau formations, tall and flat-topped, spread across the surface in clusters, and the plant growth that coats them in patterns geometric enough to catch your eye from a distance. The atmosphere is toxic. Surface work means sealed suits, and the low-lying gas clouds that collect between the plateaus will kill without filtration gear.

The only settlement is Beacon, an orbital station running mining operations on the plateau surfaces. Getting anything useful off Holland is slow. The native plant life attacks automated equipment often enough that most crews keep their surface rotations short.

It's not on most trade routes. Ships that stop here are supplying Beacon or have business deeper in the Outlands.
